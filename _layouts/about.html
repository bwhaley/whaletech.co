About

WhaleTech is a consortium of independent consultants. We work with a wide assortment of businesses, from stealth mode startups to the Fortune 500, to solve challenging infrastructure problems.

An abbreviated list of consulting services:

  • Cloud infrastructure design, implementation, and automation
  • Vulnerability assessments and infrastructure review
  • M&A due diligence, vulnerability analysis, and vendor assessments
  • Configuration management systems
  • Continuous delivery pipelines
  • Immutable infrastructure
  • Containers and orchestration
  • Telemetry: monitoring, visualization, and alerting, logging
  • Custom training on Linux, security, operations, system administration and open source software

Ben Whaley is the founder of WhaleTech. He was a central member of the technical team behind Apigee Edge, a globally-distributed API management platform built on AWS (now owned by Google). He helped to build and operate the services for Anki, a robotics and artificial intelligence company based in San Francisco.

Ben was selected as one of the first Amazon Web Services Community Heroes. Ben is a co-author of the UNIX and Linux System Administration Handbook and other educational works on system administration. He maintains the CISSP credential, and was previously an RHCE and a PCI QSA.
